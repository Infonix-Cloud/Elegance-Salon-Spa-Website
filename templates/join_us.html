Our Hiring Process

Application

Submit your application online or in person. We review all applications and respond within 5-7 business days.

Phone Interview

Initial conversation to discuss your experience, interests, and the position requirements.

In-Person Interview

Meet with our salon manager and team lead to discuss your approach and philosophy.

Technical Evaluation

Depending on the position, you may be asked to demonstrate your skills through a practical assessment.

Offer & Onboarding

Selected candidates will receive an offer and begin our comprehensive training program.
